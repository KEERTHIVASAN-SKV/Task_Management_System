SOLUTION:
---------
Clear your browser cache using ONE of these methods:

METHOD 1 - HARD REFRESH (RECOMMENDED):
   Windows/Linux: Press Ctrl + Shift + R
   Mac: Press Cmd + Shift + R
   
   Do this while on the index.html page!

METHOD 2 - USE DEBUG PAGE:
   1. Open: frontend/debug.html
   2. Click "Clear Cache & Reload"
   3. Then open: frontend/index.html

METHOD 3 - INCOGNITO MODE:
   1. Open browser in Incognito/Private mode
   2. Open: frontend/index.html
   3. Should work immediately

METHOD 4 - CLEAR BROWSER CACHE:
   1. Browser Settings → Clear browsing data
   2. Select "Cached images and files"
   3. Clear and reload
